How to Breed Betta Fish

Breeding bettas is an art and science. Follow these steps for high success rates, integrated with video tutorials and product links.

Step 1: Choose a Compatible Pair +
  • Select healthy bettas aged 4-12 months.
  • Condition with protein-rich foods for 1-2 weeks.
  • Look for flaring, color changes, and female bars.
Pro Tip: Healthy pairs increase spawn viability by 50%.
Shop Conditioning Foods
Step 2: Prepare the Breeding Tank +
  • 5-10 gallon tank.
  • 80-86°F temperature.
  • 5-6 inches water depth.
  • No gravel; gentle filtration.
  • Floating plants for nests.
Get Breeding Tank Essentials
Step 3: Introduce & Spawn +

Use a chimney or container for safe introduction. Monitor for wrapping embrace.

Watch: Step-by-Step Spawning Guide

Step 4: Post-Spawn Care +

Remove female post-spawn; male guards eggs. Hatch in 24-48 hours.

FAQ

How long for eggs to hatch? +

24-48 hours at 80-86°F.

When do fry swim free? +

2-3 days post-hatch.

When separate male? +

Once fry swim free.

How many eggs? +

30-500 per spawn.

Why fry dying? +

Poor water, wrong food, temp issues.
